BEACHES

Diveagar

Nestled along the Konkan coast, Diveagar is a serene beach with golden sands and lush greenery. This hidden gem offers horse rides along the shoreline, scenic sunsets, and peaceful vibes for nature lovers. It's an ideal escape from city life, promising tranquility and breathtaking views.

Tarkarli

Famous for its crystal-clear waters and white sands, Tarkarli is a paradise for water sports enthusiasts. From snorkeling and scuba diving to jet skiing and parasailing, this beach has it all. Its coral reefs and marine life make it a top choice for adventure and relaxation.

Shrivardhan

Shrivardhan is a peaceful beach town known for its pristine coastline and calm waters. With white sands and beautiful sunsets, it’s perfect for long walks and relaxation. The town’s rich history adds charm to this destination, making it ideal for a quiet getaway.

Velneshwar

A crescent-shaped beach lined with coconut trees, Velneshwar offers calm waters and stunning views. Known for its scenic beauty, it’s a great spot for swimming and sunbathing. Away from commercial hustle, this beach ensures peace and serenity.

Bordi Dahanu

Bordi Dahanu is an offbeat gem with a 17-kilometer stretch of coastline bordered by lush greenery. The peaceful ambiance and clean surroundings make it an ideal place to unwind. Its rural charm and calm waters offer a refreshing experience away from city chaos.

Ganpatipule

Home to one of Maharashtra's most famous white sand beaches, Ganpatipule is a blend of spirituality and natural beauty. The beach is located near the Swayambhu Ganpati temple, attracting thousands of devotees and tourists every year. Perfect for family trips and peaceful holidays.
