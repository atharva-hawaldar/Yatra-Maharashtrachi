Bordi Dahanu BeachStretching along Maharashtra’s Palghar coastline, about 145 km north of Mumbai, the Bordi–Dahanu corridor offers a nearly 6 km expanse of soft, golden sand, bordered by lush chikoo (sapodilla) orchards that give the region its unique agro-coastal charm. This seaside agrarian landscape is dotted with quaint villages and tranquil farm lanes, making it a peaceful retreat away from the bustle. Getting There & Access: The region is accessible by Western Railway (Bordi Road & Dahanu Road stations) or state buses from Mumbai and Thane. Last-mile travel includes shared autos or rickshaws—beach drop-offs can get you directly to the dunes and orchards, though some homestays may require a short walk. Accommodation & Local Living: Options include elegant farm stays nestled in chikoo groves, restored heritage Parsi homes transformed into guesthouses, and budget beach lodges. Many hosts serve homemade chikoo-based dishes—smoothies, ice creams, and baked delights—as well as fresh catch-of-the-day meals seasoned with regional spices. Activities & Exploration:
1.Chikoo Orchard Tours during the January Dahanu Chikoo Festival, where visitors can pick fruit, stroll through orchards, and enjoy local performances.
2.Farm-to-table experiences, with orchard owners demonstrating fruit processing and offering seasonal tasting sessions.
3.Beachside leisure, perfect for sunrise walks, shell collecting, or gentle horse-cart rides through palm and orchard-lined lanes.
4.Adventure options like paragliding (seasonal), cycling through village paths, and local artwalks showcasing Warli/Rajasthani-inspired Parsi painting on home facades.

Quick Facts

Location:
Palghar district, ~145 km from Mumbai
Shoreline:
6 km flat beach lined with chikoo orchards
Specialty:
Dahanu Chikoo Festival and farm tours
Stay:
Farm stays, Parsi heritage homes, beach lodges
Best Time:
November to February, chikoo harvest season

Activities

  • Stroll along the clean, wide beach lined with chikoo (sapodilla) orchards
  • Enjoy the calm waters for swimming and relaxing on the sandy shore
  • Taste fresh chikoo and delicious local Parsi cuisine at nearby stalls
  • Visit Bahrot Caves and experience the historic Parsi heritage
  • Shop for farm-fresh fruits and traditional Parsi sweets in local markets
