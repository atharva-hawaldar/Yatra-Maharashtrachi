Shiva Temple

Located approximately 135 kilometres away from Nagpur, Bodhalkasa is an attractive nature trail rich in biodiversity and activities. The ancient Shiva Temple is a spiritual gem amidst this natural beauty. It attracts devotees and travelers seeking peace.

Adasa Ganpati

Situated in Adasa village near Nagpur, the Adasa Ganpati is among the eight Ashtavinayaks of Vidarbha. The idol is 11 feet tall and carved from a single stone. Believed to be 4000 years old, the temple sits on a small hill, offering stunning views.

Ganpatipule Temple

This ancient temple houses a self-originated idol of Lord Ganesha, making it a significant pilgrimage spot. Facing west instead of the usual east, it overlooks the sea, creating a unique spiritual and scenic experience.

Panchganga Temple

Built at the confluence of five rivers, this temple is a major attraction in Old Mahabaleshwar. It honors rivers Krishna, Venna, Koyna, Savitri, and Gayatri and is known for its ancient architecture and spiritual significance.

Deekshabhoomi

A monumental Buddhist stupa in Nagpur, Deekshabhoomi is where Dr. B.R. Ambedkar embraced Buddhism with millions of followers in 1956. It’s one of Asia’s largest stupas and a symbol of social justice.

ISKCON Temple

The International Society for Krishna Consciousness temple in Navi Mumbai is a pristine white structure dedicated to Lord Krishna. Known for its peaceful atmosphere, it attracts devotees and tourists from around the world.
